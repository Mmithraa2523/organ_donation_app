What Happens After You Submit a Request?
  1. Our team reviews your request
  2. We search for matching blood donations in our database
  3. Priority is given based on urgency level and request date
  4. When a match is found, we'll contact you immediately
  5. You'll receive regular updates on your request status
Common Uses for Blood Transfusions
  • Surgery and trauma
  • Blood disorders like anemia or thalassemia
  • Cancer treatments
  • Severe infections or liver disease
  • Burns and heart disease
  • Childbirth complications
For Emergency Situations

If you're experiencing a medical emergency, please go directly to the nearest hospital or call emergency services. This request system is not designed for immediate emergency responses.
