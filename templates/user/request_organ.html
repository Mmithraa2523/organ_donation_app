About Organ Transplantation

The Organ Matching Process

Finding a suitable organ match involves several factors:

Blood type compatibility
Tissue matching
Organ size compatibility
Medical urgency
Waiting Period

The waiting time for an organ varies depending on several factors:

  • Your blood type
  • The availability of donor organs
  • Your medical urgency
  • Time spent on the waiting list
After Your Request is Submitted
  1. Our medical team reviews your request
  2. We search for compatible donors in our database
  3. When a potential match is found, we'll contact you immediately
  4. A doctor will be assigned to oversee your case
  5. You'll receive regular updates on your request status
