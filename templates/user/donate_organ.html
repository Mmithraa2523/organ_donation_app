Organ Donation Process
  1. Submit your donation intent through this form
  2. Our medical team reviews your application
  3. We assign a doctor to handle your donation
  4. You'll receive an OTP for verification at the donation center
  5. The assigned doctor verifies your identity using the OTP
  6. Your donated organ is matched with a compatible recipient
Eligibility Requirements
  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You should be in good general health
  • There should be no history of certain medical conditions (varies by organ)
  • Your organ must be compatible with the recipient
