Blood Donation Process
  1. Submit your donation intent through this form
  2. Our medical team reviews your application
  3. We'll contact you to schedule an appointment
  4. You'll receive an OTP for verification at the donation center
  5. The donation process takes about 10-15 minutes
  6. Your donated blood is tested, processed, and distributed to those in need
Eligibility Requirements
  • You must be at least 18 years old
  • You should weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kg)
  • You should be in good general health
  • You must wait at least 56 days between whole blood donations
  • You should not have any acute infections or illnesses
Benefits of Donating Blood
  • Save up to 3 lives with just one donation
  • Free mini-physical examination (blood pressure, pulse, temperature)
  • Reduction in risk of heart disease and cancer
  • Stimulation of blood cell production
  • Sense of contributing to your community and helping others in need
